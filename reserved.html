Frequently Asked Questions

What is MICE travel?

MICE stands for Meetings, Incentives, Conferences, and Exhibitions. In simple words, it’s all about organising official group travel, business meetings, company off-sites, reward trips, or big events for employees and clients. Instead of you handling everything separately, MICE brings it all together in one package.

Why should a company choose MICE travel?

Because it makes life so much easier for companies. Imagine planning flights, hotels, transport, food, and events all by yourself – it’s stressful and time-consuming. With MICE, everything is managed by experts, so your company can just focus on business and team bonding while the rest is taken care of.

What types of services are included in MICE?

MICE is a complete package. It usually covers flight bookings, hotel stays, cabs or buses for local travel, conference halls, event setup, sightseeing, meals, and even fun activities for the team. Basically, from start to finish, everything is handled in one smooth flow.

Can MICE be done for small teams also?

Yes, absolutely! Many people think MICE is only for huge corporates, but that’s not true. Even a small team of 10–20 people can plan a workshop, training session, or off-site trip. The size of the group doesn’t matter – what matters is creating the right experience.

Do MICE packages include international destinations?

Yes! MICE is not just limited to India. Companies often plan events in popular international spots like Dubai, Singapore, Thailand, or Europe. It adds a global touch to the experience and also makes employees excited about attending business trips.

How does MICE benefit employees?

Employees love MICE trips because they get a mix of work and relaxation. They meet colleagues outside the office, build stronger bonds, enjoy team activities, and feel motivated. Such trips often improve team spirit and overall job satisfaction.

Is MICE only about official work?

Not really. While MICE covers business activities like meetings and conferences, it also mixes in leisure. For example, a company might have training sessions during the day and team parties, sightseeing tours, or adventure activities in the evening. It’s all about balancing work with fun.

How much does MICE travel cost?

There’s no fixed price – it depends on the destination, number of people, and the type of event. Some companies go for luxury options while others prefer budget-friendly plans. The good part is that MICE packages can be fully customised to match the company’s budget and requirements.

Who takes care of the event planning in MICE?

The best part of MICE is that you don’t have to worry about a thing. A professional team (like Zenith Holidays) manages the entire process – booking flights, arranging hotels, setting up the event, handling food, and even managing emergencies. It’s like having your own travel manager.
